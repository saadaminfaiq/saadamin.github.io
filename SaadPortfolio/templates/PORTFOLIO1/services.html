My Design Process

01

Discovery

We begin with a detailed discussion about your project requirements, goals, target audience, and preferences to ensure I fully understand your vision.

02

Research

I conduct thorough research on your industry, competitors, and target audience to inform the design strategy and ensure your design stands out.

03

Concept Development

Based on our discussions and research, I create initial design concepts that align with your brand and project goals.

04

Feedback & Revision

I present the concepts for your review and gather your feedback, then make revisions to refine the design until it meets your expectations.

05

Once you're satisfied with the design, I finalize all details and prepare the files in the appropriate formats for your intended use.

06

Delivery

I deliver the final files along with any guidelines for usage, ensuring you have everything you need to implement your new design.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the design process take?

The timeline varies depending on the project complexity. Typically, logo designs take 1-2 weeks, social media posts 2-3 days, and flyers 3-5 days. I'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

How many revisions do I get?

I offer multiple revision rounds to ensure your complete satisfaction. The exact number depends on the service package, but I'm committed to working with you until you're happy with the final result.

What information do you need to get started?

To begin, I'll need details about your brand, project goals, target audience, design preferences, and any existing brand elements. The more information you provide, the better I can tailor the design to your needs.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive files in formats appropriate for your intended use, including PNG, JPG, PDF, and vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) for logos and other scalable designs.

Do you offer package deals for multiple services?

Yes, I offer discounted package deals for clients who need multiple design services. Contact me to discuss your specific needs and I'll create a custom package for you.

How do we communicate during the project?

Communication is key to a successful project. We can communicate via email, WhatsApp, or scheduled video calls, depending on your preference.
